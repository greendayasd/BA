Welcome to CrowdJump!


The goal is simple: Control the ball the left and right arrow keys and press up or space to jump and try to reach the flag!

While the game may seem pretty simple, you can submit feature requests to improve the game according to your wishes.
At the moment, every version a random idea will be chosen. You wish you could influence, what Idea will be implemented next?
You can not only submit ideas for game features but also for general things like a new voting system or a method to communicate!
