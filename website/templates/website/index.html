Welcome to CrowdJump!

The goal is simple: Control the ball the left and right arrow keys and up/space to jump and try to reach the flag!

While the game may seem pretty simple, after you beat the level you can submit a feature request.

At the moment, every version a random idea will be chosen, but you can not only submit ideas for game features but also for general things like a new voting system or a method to communicate!
